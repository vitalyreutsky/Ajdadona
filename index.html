About

Company

From modest beginnings in Thailand in 1987, Siam Canadian started off as a small trading company specializing in food products. It was during this time that Thailand’s seafood industry was entering a period of dynamic growth, fuelled by the rapid expansion of aqua-cultured shrimp. Siam Canadian saw the tremendous growth opportunities in the seafood business and thus frozen seafood soon became its core business.
